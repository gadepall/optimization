subject to $x + 2y \leq 8, 3x + 2y \leq 12, x \geq 0, y \geq 0$.\\
\textbf{Solution:} 
The given problem can be formulated as 
\begin{align}
\min_{\vec{x}} Z &= \myvec{-3 & 4}\vec{x} \\
\text{s.t.} \quad \vec{A}\vec{x} &\leq B
\end{align}
where
\begin{align}
A &= \myvec{1&2 \\ 3&2 \\ -1&0 \\ 0&-1}\\
B &= \myvec{8\\12\\0\\0}
\end{align}
By solving using cvxpy, we get
\begin{align}
\min_{\vec{x}} Z &= -12 \\
\vec{x} &= \myvec{4 \\0} 
\end{align}
\end{enumerate}
